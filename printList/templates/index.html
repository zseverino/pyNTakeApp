How it Works!
  • Find a File!
  • Submit!
  • PickUp!

Design or find a 3D model, that you want printed. If you are new to 3D modeling, you can come into the space for a quick overview on modeling, or you can use a site like Thingiverse to find models.

.stl and .3mf files are preferred.

Come in to TechLab on the 2nd floor of Cox Hall, also known as Cox Computing. There we can have a quick consultation about your print and get it started for you!

Or you can submit through our online tool! We may need to reach out for additional information.

Hours:

  • Monday -Thursday: 12 noon - 8pm
  • Friday: 12 noon - 5pm
  • Sat & Sun: Closed

We will email you when your print is finished! Additionally the status of your print will be printed under the Check Print tab above.

Come in to pick up and pay for your finished print.

Hours:

  • Monday -Thursday: 12 noon - 8pm
  • Friday: 12 noon - 5pm
  • Sat & Sun: Closed
Prices
  • PLA: $2 per 100 grams of PLA used.
  • Resin: $4 per 50 milliliters of resin used.
